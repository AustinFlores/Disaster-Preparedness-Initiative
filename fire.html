General Fire Safety Awareness

  • Install Smoke Alarms: Ensure smoke alarms are installed on every level of your home.
  • Create an Escape Plan: Plan and practice fire drills with your family.
  • Never Leave Cooking Unattended: Avoid leaving stovetops unattended while cooking.
  • Keep Fire Extinguishers Nearby: Know how to use it effectively (PASS: Pull, Aim, Squeeze, Sweep).

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What should I do if there's a fire in my home? - Immediately evacuate the building and call emergency services (dial 911 or your local fire department).
  • How can I prevent fires in my home? - Ensure that electrical wiring is properly installed and maintained.
  • What should I do if my clothes catch fire? - Stop, drop, and roll. This method helps extinguish flames.
  • How do I create a fire escape plan? - Identify two exit routes from each room and establish a designated meeting place outside.
